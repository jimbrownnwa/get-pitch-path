# Campaign: getpitchpath-launch

## Goal
Launch getpitchpath.com with a landing page that captures leads via the Quiz Funnel Scorecard and books strategy calls for high-intent prospects.

## Format
One-page landing page with dual CTA (scorecard for soft leads, strategy call for hard leads).

## Hook
"Your opt-in form converts at 2%. The quiz funnels we build convert at 30%+."

## Target Audience
Course creators, coaches, e-commerce brands, local service businesses (medspas, dental, salons), and agencies who drive traffic but convert poorly with static opt-in forms.

## Bridge to Paid Offer
Landing page demonstrates the problem (2% conversion on static forms), shows the solution (done-for-you quiz funnel system), and offers two entry points: the scorecard (lead capture + demonstration) and a strategy call (direct sales). The scorecard itself is a working quiz funnel that proves the product.

## Paid Offer
Pitchpath done-for-you quiz funnel build. Premium service pricing (scoped per project). Includes custom quiz, branching logic, funnel architecture, and complete email follow-up sequences.

## Status
draft

## Voice Notes
Direct-response operator per brand voice profile. No SaaS language. No "sign up" or "get started" CTAs. Service CTAs only: "Book a Strategy Call" and "Take the Quiz Funnel Scorecard." Honest about being new — acknowledges no case studies yet, leads with industry data instead. The transparency IS the trust signal.
