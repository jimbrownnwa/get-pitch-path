{{first_name}},

Your scorecard came back: Segmentation Opportunity.

Here's the pattern it revealed.

You have multiple offers but one funnel. Every visitor gets the same pitch, same emails, same experience — regardless of what they actually need.

That's the segmentation gap. And it's quietly costing you conversions.

Your numbers:
— Traffic: {{traffic}}
— Current conversion: {{conversion_rate}}
— Customer value: {{ltv}}

The math: You're converting at {{conversion_rate}} into {{current_leads}} leads. With proper segmentation, that number typically doubles or triples — not because you add traffic, but because you stop losing visitors who needed a different pitch than the one they got.

Here's what a segmented funnel does differently:

Instead of guessing which offer to push, a quiz asks 5-10 questions that reveal what each visitor needs, what they've tried, and what they're ready for. Then it routes each person to the right offer with the right message.

One funnel. Infinite paths.

Over the next two weeks I'll walk you through what the segmentation build looks like — and show the math on the ROI for your specific situation.
