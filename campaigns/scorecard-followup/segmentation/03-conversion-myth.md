## Email Copy

{{first_name}},

Your biggest frustration: {{frustration_verbatim}}.

Most businesses try to fix this by adding volume. More traffic. More ads. More emails.

The real fix usually isn't more. It's better routing for the volume you already have.

Quick math. Say you have 3 offers at $0, $97, and $497. Visitor arrives. Current flow: generic "download our free guide" opt-in. Everyone gets the same welcome sequence pushing the $97 product.

What happens:
— The $0 ready-to-buy visitor bounces (price too high)
— The $497 ready-to-buy visitor gets the wrong pitch (price too low)
— The $97 fit-prospect maybe converts

You optimized for the middle and lost the edges.

Now flip it. Quiz asks: "What's your situation?" Answers segment into 3 buckets. Each bucket gets routed to the right offer with the right copy.

Everyone sees the pitch that fits them.

That's the one-size-fits-all tax — and it's usually the biggest leak in a funnel like yours.

— Jim
