# Email 6: Quick Question

## Subject Line Variants

### A: "Quick question" — recommended
Safe bet. Pattern-interrupt simplicity. Feels like a one-off personal email, not a sequence email. Consistently high open rates (often 60%+) because it's nonspecific and personal-sounding.

### B: "Did I lose you?"
Bold play. Direct, vulnerable, slightly confrontational. High open rate but may feel needy. Reserve for segments with strong existing engagement signals (opened previous 2+ emails).

### C: "hey — still thinking it over?"
Personal. Lowercase, casual, assumes a warm prior relationship. Best for creator/coach segments or any lead who has clicked at least once earlier in the sequence.

**Recommended A/B test:** A vs B
**Reason:** Final sequence email — optimize for reply rate, not open rate. Test whether generic-personal (A) or vulnerable-direct (B) earns more replies. Reply data signals which style to use for future re-engagement campaigns.
