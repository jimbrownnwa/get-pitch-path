# Email 5: Open Spot

## Subject Line Variants

### A: "Got a spot opening next week" — recommended
Safe bet. Honest scarcity (not manufactured) — Pitchpath genuinely has limited build capacity. Informational not aggressive. High open rate because it implies something concrete is available.

### B: "Want it or pass?"
Bold play. Direct, almost confrontational. Forces a decision. High risk — may feel pushy to lower-urgency leads. Best reserved for tier:goldmine + frustration:leads/conversion (already-motivated profiles).

### C: "{{first_name}} — quick one on timing"
Personal. Frames the email as a question rather than a pitch. Lower open rate than A but higher reply rate — useful if reply engagement is the secondary metric.

**Recommended A/B test:** A vs B
**Reason:** Tests honest-scarcity (A) against direct-ask (B). Signal: does the Goldmine segment respond to soft availability or hard asks? If B wins, future sequences can tighten the ask. If A wins, the current approach is calibrated correctly.
