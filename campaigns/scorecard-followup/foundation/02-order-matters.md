## Email Copy

{{first_name}},

Quick story.

Most businesses that try to build a quiz funnel too early end up with a beautiful funnel and no traffic to drive through it. I've seen it happen — a founder invests 3 months on a quiz funnel, launches, then watches 8 visitors trickle in per week.

The problem wasn't the funnel. It was the order.

Conversion tools amplify what's already working. If nothing is working, you get a 15% conversion rate on 10 visitors/mo. A great conversion rate on a tiny denominator.

That's why Foundation Builder results get a different email plan than Goldmine results.

For your stage, the money move is:

1. Find a channel that drives at least 1,000 visitors/mo consistently
2. Pick 1-2 offers (not 5-10)
3. Then — and only then — build the conversion system

Do those first two, and the quiz funnel becomes the force multiplier it's supposed to be.

Over the next few emails I'll share specific things to focus on while you build toward that.

— Jim
