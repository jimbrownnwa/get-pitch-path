## Email Copy

{{first_name}},

One thing to focus on this month: hit 1,000 visitors/mo consistently on one channel.

Not five channels. One.

Pick whichever fits your business:

— Content (SEO blog posts or YouTube) if you're patient and have things to teach
— Organic social (LinkedIn, Twitter, IG) if you can post 4-5x/week
— Paid (Meta, Google) if you have a budget and a clear offer
— Cold outreach if you're B2B and can stand rejection

Go deep on one. Ignore the others for 90 days.

Why this matters: 1,000 visitors/mo is the point where conversion optimization starts to pay off. Below that, even a great quiz funnel doesn't move the needle enough to justify the build. Above that, the math starts working fast.

Track one metric: monthly sessions from your chosen channel. Everything else is noise at this stage.

— Jim
