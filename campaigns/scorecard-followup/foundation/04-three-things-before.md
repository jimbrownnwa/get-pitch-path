## Email Copy

{{first_name}},

If a quiz funnel is on your 90-day plan, here are the three things to build before it.

**1. One reliable traffic source.**
1,000 visitors/mo minimum. If you can't describe exactly where your traffic comes from in one sentence, start here. Without this, nothing else matters.

**2. Two or three distinct offers.**
Segmentation needs something to segment toward. If you only have one offer, a quiz funnel is overkill — a simple opt-in works fine. Build at least 2-3 offers at different price points before worrying about routing.

**3. A customer value number you can quote.**
Average customer worth. LTV. If you don't know this number, you can't do the ROI math on a quiz funnel. Track every sale for 60 days, then divide.

If all three are in place, you're no longer a Foundation Builder — retake the scorecard. If they're not, work on them in order.

Pitchpath is here when you're ready.

— Jim

**P.S.** None of these three require hiring, spending big, or learning anything new. They're discipline moves, not skill moves.
