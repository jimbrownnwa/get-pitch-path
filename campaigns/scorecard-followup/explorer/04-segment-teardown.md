## Email Copy

{{first_name}},

Quick teardown — what a quiz funnel looks like across 5 business types.

**Course creators / coaches**
"Which program is right for you?" — routes beginners to a starter course, advanced learners to a flagship, high-ticket prospects to a call.

**E-commerce / DTC**
"Find your perfect {{product}}" — reduces choice paralysis, routes to a specific SKU or bundle, increases AOV.

**Medspas / local service**
"What treatment is right for you?" — qualifies by concern, budget, timeline. Pre-warms the lead before they book.

**Agencies / B2B services**
"How does your {{marketing/ops}} score?" — diagnostic scorecard (like this one) that positions the agency as the expert and pre-qualifies the sales call.

**Financial / professional services**
"What's your {{readiness/stage}} for X?" — segments visitors by readiness, routes high-fit leads to a consult, gives low-fit leads a nurture path.

Pattern across all of them: the quiz isn't just the lead capture. It's the first sales conversation. The better the quiz, the higher down the funnel the lead lands when they hit the call.

Your business type from the scorecard: {{segment_label}}. The blueprint above for {{segment_noun}}s is roughly what yours would look like.
