## Email Copy

{{first_name}},

Quick primer — what a quiz funnel actually is, and what it isn't.

**A quiz funnel IS:**
— An interactive quiz that serves as the lead capture mechanism
— Branching logic that routes visitors to different result paths
— Personalized result pages that read as diagnostic, not generic
— Follow-up email sequences that reference each lead's specific answers
— A complete system: quiz → results → emails → offer → booking

**A quiz funnel ISN'T:**
— A BuzzFeed-style personality test
— A Typeform with a pretty skin
— A magic conversion machine that works without strategy
— A substitute for a good offer or real traffic

A quiz funnel is a sales conversation, automated. The quiz does the qualifying. The results do the personalizing. The emails do the selling.

The math: static opt-in forms convert at 1-3%. Well-built quiz funnels convert at 15-30%.

That's the delta. Everything in the business model flows from it.

More examples in the next email.

— Jim
