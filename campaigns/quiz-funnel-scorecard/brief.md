# Campaign: Quiz Funnel Scorecard

## Goal
Capture qualified leads for Pitchpath's done-for-you quiz funnel service. Target: 15%+ opt-in rate on the scorecard itself (eating our own cooking). Secondary: segment all leads by business type, score tier, and urgency for personalized follow-up.

## Format
Interactive quiz (11 questions, 5 result profiles, segment-specific blueprints)

## Hook
"Is Your Business Leaving Money on the Table Without a Quiz Funnel? Find Out in 2 Minutes."

## Target Audience
Course creators, coaches, e-commerce brands, local service businesses (medspas, dental, salons), and agencies who drive traffic but convert poorly with static opt-in forms. Common thread: they have something to sell and traffic to drive, but their lead capture is underperforming.

## Bridge to Paid Offer
The quiz demonstrates the exact product Pitchpath sells. The prospect experiences a quiz funnel firsthand, sees how segmentation and personalization work, and receives a custom blueprint for their business. The bridge is experiential, not just logical — they FEEL the product before being asked to buy it.

## Paid Offer
Pitchpath done-for-you quiz funnel build — includes custom quiz, funnel architecture, branching logic, and complete email follow-up sequences. High-ticket service (pricing TBD).

## Competitive Differentiation
Every competitor in the quiz funnel space uses PDFs, free trials, and template libraries as lead magnets. Nobody uses a quiz funnel to sell quiz funnels. This is the only lead magnet in the market that IS the product demo. Additionally, the personalized blueprint and segment-specific follow-up emails are more valuable than any generic PDF guide.

## Distribution Plan
- Landing page at getpitchpath.com (primary conversion point)
- Paid social ads (Meta, LinkedIn) driving directly to quiz
- Organic social content promoting the scorecard
- Content marketing / SEO driving to quiz
- Cold outreach to niche verticals (medspas, course creators) with quiz link
- Partner/affiliate promotion

## Status
draft

## Voice Notes
Direct-response operator voice per brand voice profile. Results copy should feel like a senior consultant reading your numbers and giving you a straight answer — not a chatbot summary. Use specific numbers from the quiz taker's answers in the result copy (e.g., "At your traffic level of X visitors/month..."). Never use SaaS language. CTA is always "book a call" or "see our work" for high-score profiles, nurture-first for low-score.
